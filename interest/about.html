Hello from an ordinary New Yorker

Escape the concrete jungle of New York City and take a break from the fast-paced lifestyle with a rejuvenating getaway like no other! As a fellow New Yorker who understands the pressures of city living, I'm Haiqi Zeng, an ordinary college student living in NYC who enjoys making plans for trips (and sometimes manages to find the time to make them come true lol). Imagine trading the honking horns for the sound of crashing waves, and the crowded streets for serene nature trails. Leave the stress behind as you embark on a getaway that will refresh your mind, body, and soul. Whether you're craving a beach retreat, a mountain adventure, or a tranquil countryside escape, I would like to share with you some of the getaway ideas that will help you recharge and be ready to take on the world again. Say goodbye to the hustle and hello to a well-deserved retreat!
