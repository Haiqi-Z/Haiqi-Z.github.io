Human Rights Crisis behind Cobalt

Cobalt is an essential mineral used in batteries for electric vehicles, smartphones, and laptops. Cobalt mining is linked to numerous human rights abuses, including the use of child labor, forced labor, and exploitation of vulnerable workers.

Environmental Impact of Lithium

Lithium mining can have significant environmental impacts, including endangering wildlife such as flamingos and disruption of fragile ecosystems. The extraction process results in the depletion of local water resources and the contamination of water sources, affecting the flamingos' natural habitats.

Learn More

Devastated Open-pit Copper Mining

Open-pit copper mining can have severe environmental impacts, including the destruction of natural habitats, soil and water pollution, and emission of greenhouse gases. The process involves the excavation of large areas of land, which can disrupt ecosystems and lead to habitat loss for wildlife.

Ethical Mineral Monitor

Welcome to Ethical Mineral Monitor - the premier online resource for raising awareness of the environmental and social impacts of mineral extraction.

Our mission is to provide information and resources to help individuals understand the impacts of mineral extraction on the environment, communities, and workers. We believe that everyone has a role to play in creating a more sustainable future, and that education and awareness are key to making informed decisions.

At Ethical Mineral Monitor, we provide articles, news stories, and other resources that shed light on the current problems in the mining industry, including environmental degradation, human rights violations, and exploitation of workers. We also provide information on the latest developments in sustainable mining practices. Our goal is to help individuals understand the real cost of the products they use and consume, and to empower them to make informed decisions about their consumption choices.

At Ethical Mineral Monitor, we believe that everyone can make a difference in creating a more sustainable future for our planet. That's why we offer a variety of volunteer opportunities for individuals who want to get involved in our mission. As a volunteer with Ethical Mineral Monitor, you can help us create content, manage our social media channels, assist with research, or participate in outreach and educational events. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels, and we are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for all members of our team.

Join us in our mission to raise awareness of the impacts of mineral extraction. Together, we can create a more sustainable future for our planet and its inhabitants.
