Tre Giovani

Location: 548 LaGuardia Pl, New York, NY 10012

3 Giovani is a cozy, contemporary Italian stalwart nestled in the quaint Greenwich Village neighborhood of Manhattan, just a block from Washington Square Park. Here, authentic Tuscan cuisine and fervent hospitality come together to bring you an unforgettable dining experience.

I highly recommend to try its Linguine “Nero” (seafood pasta) and its Burrata pizza!

Ja Moy Kitchen & Bar

Location: 208 Thompson St, New York, NY 10012

The name Ja Moy means" Have a meal" in chef's hometown. The chef learned the craft at a "hometown culinary school" since a kid, and travelled to many countries as possible to absorb their culinary cultures, cooking skills. All dishes are cooked by the chef herself, using the best ingredients she chooses from the suppliers.

I highly recommend to try the Braised Rice with Pork and Soy Sauce and Salt baked chicken! They are amazing!

Gyu-Kaku Japanese BBQ

Location: 34 Cooper Sq, New York, NY 10003

Gyu-Kaku, meaning "Horn of the Bull" in Japanese, provides authentic Japanese yakiniku (grilled barbecue) dining experience where customers share premium cooked meats over a flaming smokeless grill, while sipping on Japanese sake, shochu, and frosty cold beers.

I highly recommend its lunch special! If you go with a friend, you will be able to try all six kinds of beef it has. $19.95 + tips + tax for a meal set with three choices of protein, soup, rice and salad. What a deal!
