Jacky Cheung Hok-yau, born on July 10, 1961, in Hong Kong, is a highly acclaimed Cantopop singer and actor. Jacky Cheung has released numerous albums throughout his career, and his music covers a wide range of genres, including pop, ballads, and love songs. He is known for his powerful and emotive vocals, which have earned him a dedicated fan base.Jacky Cheung is also known for his legendary live concerts. His "1/2 Century Tour" in 2018, celebrating his 30th anniversary in the music industry, broke attendance records and showcased his enduring popularity.

Among all his songs, I love "Li Xiang Lan" the most. This song is particularly well-known for its emotional and nostalgic lyrics. It's one of Jacky Cheung's iconic songs and has been cherished by his fans and music enthusiasts for many years.
