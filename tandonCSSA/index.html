NYU Tandon CSSA (NYU Tandon Chinese Student Scholars Association)

NYU Tandon CSSA is a non-profit organization, comprised primarily of Chinese students from NYU Tandon School of Engineering and other schools at NYU. Based in the center of Brooklyn Tech Triangle, we serve all Chinese students at NYU, and are dedicated to providing high-quality academic and living resources. Additionally, we strive to promote cultural and academic exchange between Chinese and American scholars.

Events

NYU Tandon CSSA hosts a variety of exciting events throughout the academic year, aimed at creating opportunities for Chinese international students to socialize, have fun, and connect with their peers. From speed friending to singing competitions, picnics to cultural festivals, our events cater to a range of interests and backgrounds. We are committed to fostering a vibrant community within NYU Tandon and the Brooklyn Tech Triangle, and invite all students to join us in celebrating Chinese culture and building lasting friendships.
